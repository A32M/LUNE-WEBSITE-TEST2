About LUNE

LUNE is a Dallas-based coffee cart designed around soft aesthetics, intentional details, and memorable coffee experiences.


From intimate gatherings to elevated events, every cup is crafted with care to create a warm and inviting atmosphere.


Elegant tones, handcrafted drinks, and a refined mobile setup make LUNE a unique addition to celebrations, weddings, and community events.
